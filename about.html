About


Hello there, I'm Miguel Bracamontes. I use the internet handle @bracamontesman everywhere and @nyctrax when I'm playing videogames. I'm a 30-year-old self-taught tech enthusiast running this website, which is basically all about me and the things I like. I have a bachelor's degree in linguistics, but I have a computer for a heart. I used to have an account on every major social network, and bits of it are forever cached on Google and the Internet Archive, so my old photo is kind of like an Easter egg.

There have been several other iterations of this blog, but they were written in other language and are no longer active. Their memories will only live on in the Wayback Machine.

I prioritize authenticity and simplicity, avoiding any use of content generation and AI.
