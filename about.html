About


Hello there, I'm Miguel Bracamontes. I use the internet handle @bracamontesman everywhere and @nyctrax when I'm playing videogames. I'm a 30-year-old self-taught tech enthusiast running this website, which is basically all about me and the things I like. I'm not claiming to be an expert in any of the topics I write about, but I give it my all to stick to the facts and truth, and I try to share my sources whenever I can.

There have been several other iterations of this blog, but they were written in other languages and are no longer active. Their memories will only live on in the Wayback Machine.

I have a bachelor's degree in linguistics, but I have a computer for a heart. I used to have an account on every major social network, and bits of it are forever cached on Google and the Internet Archive, so my old photo is kind of like an Easter egg.

My political convictions include decentralization, voluntary cooperation, and mutual aid. Hierarchies and oppressive power structures are to be abolished by empowering our communities to govern themselves without coercion or domination. I believe in freedom and libertarianism.

I prioritize authenticity and simplicity, avoiding any use of content generation and AI.
